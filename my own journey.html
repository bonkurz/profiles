    Introduction

    I fell in love with Hip Hop at a very young age. When I was 11, I watched 8 Mile and was immediately fascinated by how quckly those guys can think on their feet.
    In my early years of high school, my fascination quickly became an obsesion that would shape the way I see the world and even how I related to people.
    I'm your typical old school boombap rapper with a few modern elements but I will always prefer 80's - 90's Hip Hop over what we have today.
    I used to hold cyphers almost everyday at the Vosloo stadium with my friends, until we formed a group called I 3rd World where I recorded my first song on a dowloaded beat, titled Dominion.



    I 3rd World would go on to dominate the Vosloo Hip Hop scene until we had a falling out in December of 2016, that's when I started focusing on solo projects.
    That's how my EP Initiate The Word and the pjoject #GoBonkurz came to be.

    Growing Emcee

    The falling of I 3rd World led me to focus on my solo career, which eventually led to the birth of Initiate The Word and #GoBonkurz.

    Shortly after that, I regrouped with some I 3rd World members and started working on Dojo, a new group that was meant to fix all the mistakes we made as I 3rd World.

    Dojo had initially been my idea way before that, so everyone else just joined me and that's how we roll till this day.
